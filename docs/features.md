# App Features

## Player vs Player (Important)
- Players use a standard physical chessboard.
- The camera captures each move and records the full game.
- After the game, users can replay moves and explore alternate scenarios.
- The app provides direct feedback and generates a **PGN** file for use with external analysis tools.

---

## Player vs AI (Important)
- Users play chess over the board against an AI opponent.
- The AI announces its moves in real time through the app or speaker.
- *(Optional)* The AI may include personality modes or varying play styles.
- Designed to improve over-the-board confidence and square recognition.

---

## Player Learning Mode (Less Likely)
- Users can take lessons directly on the physical board.
- The system presents scenarios or tactics for the user to solve.
- The camera detects the player's moves and provides real-time corrections.
- Creates a more interactive and engaging over-the-board learning experience.
